Welcome!

Have you ever wanted to design a board game? Or, seen a cool game on a store shelf and said to yourself: “I wonder how they came up with that?”

ATX Game Designers is a board and card game incubator and social club. It’s a place where game designers and board game enthusiasts can meet to explore board game mechanics, themes, playing styles, and everything in between. Whether you’ve tinkered with a game idea, pitched and published games, or are passionate about sharing your insights to make good projects better, we’d love to help refine ideas and find the fun in the process.

Come make great games with us!
